> Why assume that the neutral point of the wing is 25% of the chord.

This is the first estimate, remember that this results is based on
a very thin airfoil in potenatial flow.

Actual airfoils in viscous flow will show a neutral point slightly
different from quarter chord.  Further, it is only a "point" when
CL and Cm are constant functions.
